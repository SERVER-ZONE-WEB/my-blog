What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity involves protecting internet-connected systems, including hardware, software, and data, from cyber threats. It is essential for individuals and businesses to stay protected from potential cyberattacks...

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing Attacks
  • Malware and Ransomware
  • Data Breaches
  • Denial-of-Service (DoS) Attacks
  • Social Engineering

How to Stay Safe Online

To ensure cybersecurity, follow these best practices:

  • Use strong and unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Keep software and operating systems updated
  • Avoid suspicious emails and links
  • Use a reliable antivirus and firewall
